About Lauren

Lauren is a New York City-based personal stylist. A New Hampshire native with a lifelong love of clothes, she studied Communications and Fashion Merchandising at Marist College. After spending nearly ten years working in Marketing and Communications, Lauren’s love of styling was reignited after helping a friend revamp her wardrobe for a new job, and thus, 32 Fifth was born!


Lauren is a Bridal Stylist at BHLDN and is pursuing a Personal Style Certificate from The New York Institute of Art & Design.
